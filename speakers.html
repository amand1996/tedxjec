SPEAKERS

Aditya Pratap Singh

Aditya Pratap Singh is a first generation Entrepreneur. Having done his Bachelors in INDUSTRIAL PRODUCTION Engineering (Hons.) he has embarked on a journey very difficult to travel i.e, Entrepreneurship. Currently, he is the co-founder of his very second business venture called SharpNode. He is also a National Level Volleball player and believes that physical fitness is mutual to mental. He also mentors various other start-ups and promotes entrepreneurship. He has been an inspiration as recent alumni and believes strongly in networking and contact building. He looks after the Finance and Marketing sector to foster the growth of his startup. He has inclined interests in R&D and has been the author of a research paper at the International Journal of Research.

Pavitra Shetty

Pavitra Shetty or 'Pavi' as she prefers to be called for reasons of social acceptance is an IT engineer, infrequent gym-goer and burger enthusiast, all redeeming qualities for the average non specific, she just happens to be a girl. The biggest inspirations for her stand-up are her mom, random aunts who keep visiting her place and her very own weighing scale. She juggles her full-time IT job with 200 best friends and still finds humour enough in life to make it to stand-up shows around the city of Mumbai. Pavitra headlines Chalta Hai Comedy's most popular show 'Undateable' and has been featured on TV, podcasts, radio and in print multiple times. She will talk about how humor emerges from an organic process (not the one you think) and how it helps people come to terms with imperfections and the small disappointments of everyday life.

Raul Villamarin Rodriguez

Raul is a Criminal Intelligence Officer at Interpol, Neuroscientist, Psychophysiologist & is rated Asia's Top Expert in the Science of Detecting Deception and among the Top 25 Globally. He is a recognized authority on Deception Detection, Psychophysiology, Fraud Examination, Business Communication, Fraud Analysis, Analysis Of Emotions, Emotional Footprinting, Criminal Profiling and Analytical interviewing. He provides training and consulting services for national law enforcement, Interpol, government agencies, large corporations and firms throughout Europe and Asia. Raul is Certified Master Anti-Terrorism Specialist (CMAS) and holds a diploma as a Neuro-Linguistic Programming Practioner by American Hypnosis Association; Certified Deception Expert by Paul Ekman Group;Certified Fraud Examiner by the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners. He has relevant experience in several cases regarding renowned criminal and terrorist organizations such as D-Company, Boko Haram, and ISIS.

Swarupa Ananth

She is one half of the Ethnotronic collaborative, Filter Coffee, who organically combine virtuosic improvisation with electronic soundscapes and hypnotic grooves. Ethnotronic – Ethnic and Electronic is Filter Coffee’s own signature brew that offers a new look at Classical/Folk music through a modern electronic perspective. Having spent the most part of the last two years touring and playing music festivals around the world the duo also successfully released their debut album “Raagatronic” ranking number one on the iTunes India music charts.

Varun Jain

Varun Jain is an otaku, who's working as an associate analyst for ernst and young. On a personal level, he believes in taking risks and making mistakes. He is firm believer of learning through mistakes. It was only after making mistakes, stumbling, stuttering and having nervous spells that Varun learnt to conquer the institution that is Model United Nations. He set up MUNation in 2015. Since then, neither him, nor his organization has looked back. They aim to introduce a culture of talking it out through panel discussions, to promote problem solving action. This is done through innovations like an MUN magazine. He hopes high of organizing an international MUN in India, and learnt karate to defend his dream. Other than kicking in the air, Varun spends his time playing guitar, going to gym and travelling.
