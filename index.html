What is TEDx?

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDx[name], where x = independently organized TED event. At our TEDx[name] event, TEDTalks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.

About the speaker

Pavitra Shetty or 'Pavi' as she prefers to be called for reasons of social acceptance is an IT engineer, infrequent gym-goer and burger enthusiast, all redeeming qualities for the average non specific, she just happens to be a girl. The biggest inspirations for her stand-up are her mom, random aunts who keep visiting her place and her very own weighing scale. She juggles her full-time IT job with 200 best friends and still finds humour enough in life to make it to stand-up shows around the city of Mumbai. Pavitra headlines Chalta Hai Comedy's most popular show 'Undateable' and has been featured on TV, podcasts, radio and in print multiple times. She will talk about how humor emerges from an organic process (not the one you think) and how it helps people come to terms with imperfections and the small disappointments of everyday life.

About the speaker

Varun Jain is an otaku, who's working as an associate analyst for ernst and young. On a personal level, he believes in taking risks and making mistakes. He is firm believer of learning through mistakes. It was only after making mistakes, stumbling, stuttering and having nervous spells that Varun learnt to conquer the institution that is Model United Nations. He set up MUNation in 2015. Since then, neither him, nor his organization has looked back. They aim to introduce a culture of talking it out through panel discussions, to promote problem solving action. This is done through innovations like an MUN magazine. He hopes high of organizing an international MUN in India, and learnt karate to defend his dream. Other than kicking in the air, Varun spends his time playing guitar, going to gym and travelling.
